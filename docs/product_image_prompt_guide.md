# 🖼️ EIGHTY FIVE ELEVEN — Product Image Prompt Guide

Use this prompt every time you create or generate a product image to keep the entire catalog visually cohesive.

---

## 📐 Specs At-A-Glance

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| **Dimensions** | 1000 × 1000 px (1:1 square) |
| **Format** | PNG (transparent) or JPG (white bg) |
| **Max file size** | < 500 KB |
| **Background** | Solid pure white `#FFFFFF` or transparent |
| **Subject fill** | ~70-80% of the frame |
| **Lighting** | Soft, even, top-down studio light |
| **Shadow** | Subtle natural drop shadow directly beneath the product |

---

## 🔥 Universal Master Prompt

> [!TIP]
> Copy-paste this prompt into any AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL·E, Firefly, etc.) and replace `[PRODUCT NAME]` with your item.

```
High-end e-commerce product photograph of [PRODUCT NAME], centered on a pure white seamless background, shot from a 3/4 front angle. Soft diffused studio lighting from above with no harsh shadows. The product fills approximately 75% of the square frame. Minimal natural drop shadow directly beneath the product for depth. Clean, sharp focus with high detail on textures, stitching, and materials. Professional commercial product photography style, no props, no lifestyle elements, no text overlays. 1000x1000 pixels, square format.
```

## 👟 Category-Specific Variants

### Sneakers / Footwear
```
High-end e-commerce product photograph of [SNEAKER NAME], single shoe positioned at a 3/4 front-lateral angle showing the outer side profile, toebox, and partial sole. Centered on a pure white seamless background. Laces neatly tied and naturally draped. Soft diffused studio lighting from above, subtle natural drop shadow beneath the sole. Sharp focus on textures, leather grain, mesh weave, and stitching details. Clean commercial product photography, no props, no text. Square format, 1000x1000px.
```

### Apparel (Hoodies, Tees, Jackets)
```
High-end e-commerce product photograph of [GARMENT NAME], laid flat or ghost mannequin style showing the full front of the garment, centered on a pure white seamless background. Fabric naturally relaxed with clean folds. Soft diffused studio lighting from above with no harsh shadows. Sharp focus on fabric texture, print detail, and tag/label if visible. Subtle natural drop shadow for depth. Clean commercial product photography, no model, no props, no text. Square format, 1000x1000px.
```

### Accessories (Hats, Bags, Belts)
```
High-end e-commerce product photograph of [ACCESSORY NAME], centered on a pure white seamless background, shot from a 3/4 front angle. Soft diffused studio lighting from above. The product fills approximately 70% of the square frame. Sharp focus on material textures, hardware, and branding details. Minimal natural drop shadow beneath. Clean commercial product photography, no props, no lifestyle, no text. Square format, 1000x1000px.
```

## ✅ Do's and Don'ts

### Do ✅
- Keep the background **pure white** (#FFFFFF) every time
- Center the product with **even padding** on all sides
- Show **texture details** — leather grain, mesh weave, fabric knit
- Use the **same camera angle** per category (3/4 front for shoes, flat-lay for apparel)
- Include a **subtle drop shadow** for grounding

### Don't ❌
- No colored, gradient, or lifestyle backgrounds
- No watermarks, logos, or text overlays on the image
- No props, plants, or decorative elements
- No extreme close-ups (keep the full product visible)
- No mixed lighting temperatures (warm + cool)
