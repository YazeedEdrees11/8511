## 8511 — Editorial Streetwear

A luxury sneaker boutique in Amman, Jordan. Aesthetic = high-fashion editorial meets street culture. Inspired by Off-White, Kith, A-Cold-Wall, Hidden NY.

### Visual language
- **Hi-contrast monochrome** base: paper (#F7F7F4) and ink (#0A0A0A). Generous negative space.
- **One bold accent** (#FF3B00) reserved for CTAs, hover states, the chat trigger. Never decorative.
- **Editorial grid**: 12-col, oversized hero typography (Bebas Neue, 6xl–9xl), tight tracking on headlines, wide tracking on labels (uppercase).
- **Imagery**: large product photography on neutral plates, minimum 4:3, never cropped tight. Negative space around feet/silhouettes.
- **Voice**: terse, culture-forward. Real product names. No corporate fluff. Tagline beats marketing copy.

### Components
- **Buttons**: square corners or 4px max. Solid ink fill, paper text. Or ghost (1px ink border, hover→accent). Uppercase, tracking-wider, 12-14px.
- **Product tiles**: image first (square aspect, white plate). Title in display font, all caps, tight. Price in mono/grotesk.
- **Header**: sticky, 80px tall, paper bg, 1px ink/15% bottom border. Logo left, nav center/right uppercase.
- **Chat widget**: floating accent bubble bottom-right. Uppercase label 'ASK 8511'. Opens a 380px panel with editorial type.

### Layouts
- Home: full-bleed editorial hero (60vh+), brand strip below, featured grid, services tease, store locator footer.
- Shop: dense 4-up product grid on desktop, 2-up on mobile.
- PDP: 50/50 split, image left, meta-block right with style code & colorway in monospaced detail.
- Services: numbered bilingual grid (01–08), Bebas Neue oversized numerals.
- Chat: clean conversation, product cards inline, no bubbles — editorial.
